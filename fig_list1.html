Remark2

0, 5, 10, 20 are labels with the following meaning:
label meaning color
0 Maxflow is constrainted by the generation capacity. All the n-1 nodes generate at g_tilde. purple
20 Maxflow is constrainted by the line capacity neighboring the battery. red
5 For each connected component, either case 0 or 20. blue
10 None of the above. light green
